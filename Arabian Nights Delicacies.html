Arabian Desserts

Baklava

Layered pastry filled with nuts and sweetened with honey or syrup.

kunafa

Sweet cheese pastry soaked in syrup and topped with crushed pistachios.

Umm-ali

Bread pudding made with puff pastry, milk, nuts, and raisins.

basbousa

Semolina cake soaked in sweet syrup, often flavored with coconut or rose water.
